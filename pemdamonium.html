Pemdamonium is a prototype of an order of operations platformer game, built within Unity 5. Each level begins by generating a master number to beat the level. Players run around within a level collecting digits 0-9 and operators, which are added to their backpack. They then rearrange the numerals and operators from their backpack into an equation using the equation bar at the bottom of the screen. Once they have created an equation which equals the master number, they have beaten the level.
